## What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?
Internal locus of Control is a state where a person believes that the outcome in their life is primarily the result
of their own actions, efforts and decision and not external forces like fate or luck.
### Key Points:
* Adopting an internal Locus of Control is an essential key for a long term motivation.
* While External Locus of Control is that outcomes are primarily determined by external factors like luck, fate or
any other circumstances which leads them to believe that the task or goal is beyond their personal control.
