### Paraphrase the Sleep is your Superpower video in your own words in brief. Only the points, no explanation.
* Health Risks: Short sleep increases risk for heart attacks, cancer, and reproductive problems.
* Immunity: Just one night of short sleep reduces immune cell activity by 70%.
* Memory: Lack of sleep causes a 40% deficit in the brain's ability to make new memories.
* Life Span: The shorter we sleep, the shorter life gets.
* Necessity: Sleep is a non-negotiable biological necessity, not a luxury.

### What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?
* Be Consistent: Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day to keep your body clock steady.
* Limit Stimulants: Avoid caffeine and alcohol late in the day.
* Dim lights hours before going to bed.
* No Naps: If you struggle to sleep at night, skip daytime naps.

### Paraphrase the video - Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise. Minimum 5 points, only the points.
* Feel Good Right Away: Exercise instantly makes you feel better and helps you focus.
* Grows Memory: Moving body regularly helps grow the part of our brain used for memory.
* Boosts Focus: It makes it easier for us to pay attention for longer periods of time.
* Protects from Illness: Exercise acts like a shield, making our brain strong against aging and diseases

### What are some steps you can take to exercise more?
* Start Small: Begin with just 10 minutes of walking or gentle movement.
* Tie Movement to Daily Habits: Add a specific movement like stretching to a routine event.
* Choose Stairs: Use stairs instead of the elevator.
* Find a Buddy: Exercise with a friend to make it fun and stay consistent.
* Schedule It: Write exercise time in my calendar like a must-keep appointment.
